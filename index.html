About

Thermocontroller and Relay Container

This housing is designed for safety (220V-110V relays get hot!) and for organization - for laboratory testing equipment. It contains two Eurotherm 2404 Temperature Controller/Programmers as well as two solid state relays wired to the controllers. The base is made of 1/2" plywood and the walls are made of 1/8" acrylic.

  • Client: Carbon Nanostructures Research Group, NEU
  • Date: July 2015
  • Service: Peripheral Equipment Design

Custom Straight Bracket

This straight bracket was designed as a universal fixture for connecting 40mmx40mm Robotunits, a modular building material. It was designed because of the need for a low profile connection between beds for testing robots during my time at iRobot. The brackets were machined from 5mm thick aluminum, on a CNC Machine.

  • Client: iRobot
  • Date: February 2015
  • Service: Custom Fixture Design

Electrolysis Cell

This cell was designed because of the need for an efficient, safe and less wasteful electrolysis cell. With the original cell, the 10mm2 Al foil is loaded from the bottom. This means the acidic electrolyte has to be poured out after every anodization, which could otherwise be reused. With the new, top loaded design the turnaround for anodization will be much faster. Because of the corrosive nature of oxalic acid this cell is machined from unreactive teflon.

  • Client: Carbon Nanostructures Research Group, NEU
  • Date: December 2014
  • Service: Test Equipment Design

Novel Laser Density Measurement System

This setup is a theoretical design for a system that measures the relative density of a gelatinous solution within a test tube. It implements the use of a Dragon Lasers, 655nm Laser System. It was created during my time at the USDA - Food Quality Lab, to confirm the results of other test equipment.

  • Client: USDA - Food Quality Lab
  • Date: August 2014
  • Service: Test Equipment Design
