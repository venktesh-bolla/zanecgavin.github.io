About

I am an engineering student at northeastern university, and I have a passion for engineering, computer programming, design, and for the pursuit of scientific knowledge.

I find myself frustrated by everyday objects that are poorly designed. Whether it be lack of some ergonomic feature or the presence of an unecessary feature - or worse, one that leads to misuse - I know in many cases I can do better. I have experience in a variety of fields, from scientific research, to computer vision programming, to diy home improvements and handyness. I pride myself in, not the expanse of my knowledge, but in my ability and stick-to-itiveness to acquire new knowledge and apply it.
